Given a topic and its content, perform the following tasks:

Extract Key Terms:
Identify the most important terms related to the topic. These should include concepts, technical terms, and key ideas essential to understanding the material.

Provide Definitions:
For each key term, provide a clear and concise definition. Ensure the definitions are tailored for a learning context, avoiding overly technical jargon unless necessary, and include simplified explanations or examples where appropriate.

Prioritize Clarity:
Focus on high-level terms central to the content.
Avoid including redundant or overly detailed terms that aren't critical to understanding the main points.

Output Format:
List all terms in bullet points.

For each term, use the following structure:
Key Term: Definition.

Optionally, include examples or context to enhance understanding.
Categorize key terms under relevant headings or subtopics for better organization.
